Meet The Team

AMAL ZAKIR

Design Engineer

Amal is responsible for designing the car using sketches and CAD. She ensures the car follows all competition rules while maintaining an aerodynamic shape. She also manages the team’s social media, showcasing the design process and team journey.

LAYAAN YASIR

Manufacturing Engineer

ayaan oversees the production and assembly of the car. She ensures all components are accurately made and fit together properly. She also checks measurements and weight to make sure the car meets all technical regulations.

TANVI SUNIL

Data & Testing Engineer

Tanvi focuses on analyzing performance and improving the design. She evaluates how the car behaves and identifies areas for improvement, using data and observations. She works alongside Amal, helping manage socials of the team.

HALAH BINT

Marketing Manager

Halah manages sponsorships, external relations, and car performance. She secures sponsors, communicates professionally, and monitors performance while ensuring sponsor logos are effectively displayed.
